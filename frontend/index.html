New to investing? Start with research, not guesses.

PortfolioAI groups ideas by why a beginner might look at them: what has momentum, what is usually used as a steadier core, and what the AI thinks is worth researching next. Nothing here is a buy signal; every idea has a risk check before it reaches your watchlist.
